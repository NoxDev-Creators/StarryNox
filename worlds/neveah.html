Neveah

Neveah is the dream world created by Omi. The world exists within the imagination of a child trapped in a coma, forming a landscape of toys, monsters, and dream logic.

Nature of the World

Time behaves inconsistently within Neveah. Events often feel episodic, and smaller memories fade unless they hold strong emotional meaning.

Regions

  • Dreary Meadows
  • Evergreen Hollows
  • Sugar Mountains
  • Nightmare Realm

Special Layer

Paperworld exists across all regions and can be accessed through drawings and written creations.

Main Figures
