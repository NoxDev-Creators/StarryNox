Omi

Race: Lamb Goddess

World: Neveah

Description

Omi is the central figure of the dream world of Neveah. She appears as a golden lamb with a permanent grin and large tired eyes. She is the subconscious vessel of the creator who lies in a coma, unknowingly shaping the world around her.

Abilities

Omi has the greatest power in the dream world, though she does not realize it. Her imagination shapes reality, creating lands, creatures, and events through childlike "pretend" play.

Relationships

Her closest companion is the mysterious figure known simply as Doctor, who protects her and guides her through the dream world.
