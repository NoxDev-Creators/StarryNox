Doctor

Type: Cryptid Guardian

World: Neveah

Description

Doctor is a mysterious entity who accompanies Omi throughout the dream world. He appears as a tall floating figure with two theatrical masks: one representing comedy and the other tragedy.

Role

Doctor protects Omi and encourages her imagination through games of pretend, carefully avoiding revealing the true nature of the world to her.

Only in the final chapter does he reveal his full name: Dr. Henry.
