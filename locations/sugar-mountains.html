Sugar Mountains

World: Neveah

Region Type: Frozen Treat Wonderland

Overview

The Sugar Mountains are the snowy northern biome of Neveah—but the “snow” is ice cream, and the wood is replaced with candy cane. The region feels like a frozen treat wonderland.

Inhabitants

Extra fluffy toys and creatures tend to inhabit the Sugar Mountains. If something is excessively fluffy, there’s a good chance it lives up north.

Atmosphere

The Sugar Mountains lean friendly-to-neutral overall.

Water

Frozen ponds are frozen types of milk.
