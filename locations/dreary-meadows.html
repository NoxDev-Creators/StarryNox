Dreary Meadows

World: Neveah

Region Type: Meadows / Night Hills

Overview

A wide stretch of blue grassy hills beneath a starry night sky. This was the first place formed in Neveah, where Omi wakes and Doctor meets her early on.

Notable Traits

  • Blue hills and open, breathable space
  • Star-heavy nights
  • Quiet, calm, slightly melancholy atmosphere
