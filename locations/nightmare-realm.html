Nightmare Realm

World: Neveah

Region Type: Cryptid Territory

Overview

The Nightmare Realm is a distant and isolated region of Neveah dominated by monsters and cryptids. Unlike the other biomes, it is separated by the ocean and requires a boat to reach.

Though it appears frightening to outsiders, the Nightmare Realm functions as a natural part of Neveah's ecosystem. Many monsters and cryptids inhabit this territory and govern it as their own domain.

Geography

  • Separated from the mainland by ocean
  • Accessible only by boat
  • Often perceived as darker or more ominous than the rest of Neveah

Inhabitants

  • Monsters
  • Cryptids
  • Other unusual dream entities
