Evergreen Hollows

World: Neveah

Region Type: Forest / Plushlands

Overview

A forest region shaped around comfort and safety. The Hollows are calm, soft, and lived-in—like cozy spaces inside trees with blue leaves and golden wood. This region is strongly associated with plushies and toys.

Notable Traits
